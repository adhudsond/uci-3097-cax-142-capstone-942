Employee Onboarding (IT Provisioning)

1. HR emails the IT team a PDF with the new hire's details.
2. An IT engineer reads the PDF and manually creates an Active Directory account.
3. The engineer manually creates a mailbox in the email system.
4. The engineer creates a user in the ticketing system (ServiceNow).
5. The engineer requests software licenses by emailing the procurement team.
6. Once licenses are approved (1-3 days), the engineer installs software on the laptop.
7. The engineer manually documents all credentials in a shared spreadsheet.
8. The engineer emails the hiring manager to confirm setup is complete.

Pain points: steps are sequential, heavily manual, and the 1-3 day license
approval blocks laptop preparation. Credentials are stored in a spreadsheet.
